4: General Experience

I would have to say that the most challenging experience of this for me was having to work on three sites all at once. The idea at first scared me since I felt like I wouldn’t have enough time to ensure good work on the sites along with all the other homework from other classes. But in the end, I was able to do fine since I would take the massive task before me and compartmentalize each piece into something to do each day.

I enjoyed being able to work on many different sites with different styles and being able to be creative with the “limitations” that I had and allowed me to finish the sites I was assigned.

I would probably have gone to my client and asked for a better-defined site plan sooner since that caused some issues for me.

I feel like I could work on a semi-serious website if asked of me and it being the sole thing I would need to work on. I feel more confident in my abilities in making something for someone else. I have learned how to better work with others in creating something for someone else based on their vision.
